Contractor Payout Management System
Production-Grade Functional & Technical Specification
1. Objective
The Contractor Payout Management System manages payments to contractors for completed jobs on the platform. The system ensures:
* Accurate contractor earnings calculation
* Commission and tax deductions
* Secure payout processing through bank/payment gateway
* Financial ledger tracking
* Automated and manual payout generation
* Reconciliation and auditability
The system must guarantee:
1. No job is paid more than once
2. All eligible jobs are included in payouts
3. All financial transactions are auditable

4. Job Eligibility Rules
A job qualifies for payout if:
1. Job Status = Completed
2. Customer payment status = Received
3. Job payout status = Unpaid
4. Job completion date ≤ Configured cutoff date
5. Job not included in any payout batch
Jobs are locked once included in a payout batch to prevent modification.
5. Earnings & Deduction Calculation
Job Level Earnings
Job Amount = Customer Payment
Commission = Job Amount × Commission Rate
TDS = Contractor Earnings × TDS Rate
GST on Commission = Commission × GST Rate


Net Contractor Earnings
Net Earnings = Job Amount − Commission − GST on Commission − TDS
Example:
Item
	Amount
	Job Value
	₹10,000
	Commission
	₹1,000
	GST on Commission
	₹180
	TDS (1%)
	₹90
	Net Payable
	₹8,730

	10. Payout Approval Options
Admin can approve payouts in several ways.
Full Payout
All contractors receive full payable amount.
Partial Payout
Admin specifies payout percentage.
Example:
Net Payable = ₹10,000
Payout Percentage = 60%
Payout Amount = ₹6,000
Remaining Balance = ₹4,000


Contractor Selection
Admin can select specific contractors for payout.
Cutoff Date
Only jobs completed before the specified date are included.

14. Idempotent Payment Requests
Each payout must have a unique reference ID.
Example:
PAYOUT-20260311-BATCH001


This prevents duplicate transfers if APIs retry.

	16. Failure Handling

1. Mark payout status as Failed
2. Record gateway error
3. Reverse ledger entry
